Mainvest

The Small Business Investment Marketplace . Explore the site here.

The Problem

Small businesses have limited access to capital. Any funds the can raise will be high-cost and personally guaranteed.

Apart from purchasing the good or service, local stakeholders have no way to support Small Businesses they care about.

The Solution

A two-sided marketplace where businesses fundraise and offer competitive ROIs and investors can contribute to their local economy directly and earn interest while doing so.

New Discovery Pages:

Investor Wallets

Managed the entire product release process for a 0-1 creation of investor wallets, a key goal on our product roadmap. We created custodial bank accounts for every existing user to keep the investment returns on the platform, and later gave investors the option to re-invest their returns directly.

Mainvest Repayments

Independently supervised the entire repayment process for over 300 businesses on the platform. I would organize and assist these businesses and ensure payments were made on time. As I write this, I have overseen 60k+ transactions totalling over $2MM in capital flowing back into investor wallets.
