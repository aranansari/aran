Our Mission

By building a circular marketplace, we are making the construction and DIY industry more sustainable, reducing emissions and waste across the globe. Partnering with OneTreePlanted, every transaction made on Reclaim not only contributes to the reduction of landfill waste, but will also contribute to reforestation efforts. To combat Climate Change, we understand that it will take a collective effort to reduce, reuse, and recycle.
