Privacy Policy for MixTape

At MixTape, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This privacy policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use and protect that information, and the choices users have regarding their personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information from users of our Slack App:

  • User profile information: we may collect user profile information, including names and email addresses, in order to provide our services.
  • Usage data: we may collect data on how users interact with our app, including frequency of use and which features are most frequently used.
  • Communication data: we may collect information from communications between users within our app.
  • Spotify data: we may collect information about Spotify songs, playlists, or albums that users interact with through our app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect in the following ways:

  • To provide our services and improve user experience: we use the information we collect to operate and improve our app and to personalize the user experience.
  • To communicate with users: we may use email addresses or other contact information to send important notices or updates about our app.
  • To analyze app usage: we may use the information we collect to analyze app usage and to improve our marketing efforts.
  • To personalize Spotify recommendations: we may use Spotify data to personalize recommendations and playlists for users.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share the information we collect in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers: we may share information with third-party service providers who help us operate and improve our app.
  • For legal reasons: we may share information if required by law, such as in response to a subpoena or other legal process.
  • With Slack workspaces and channels: we may share information with Slack workspaces and channels where the app is authorized to view, send, or post messages.

4. Data Security and Retention

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect from unauthorized access, use, and disclosure. We retain user data for as long as necessary to provide our services and as required by law.

5. User Rights

Users have the right to access, update, and delete their personal information. Users can also opt-out of receiving communications from us at any time.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make any material changes, we will notify users by email or within the app.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at amgansari@gmail.com.
